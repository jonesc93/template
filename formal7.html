Conclusion

Criminal Justice is a very popular field of study in America. In the academic discipline research is important in finding out information that is valuable in investigations and other projects. One of the most practiced and most valued forms of research would be reading. Reading plays a large part in the discipline of Criminal Justice. Technology also plays a significant role in Criminal Justice. Technology is tremendously imperative for police officers to perform their duties. It supports them with tasks and also makes things much easier than in the past. Technology also momentously increases productivity in the field of Criminal justice. Lastly, communication is a necessary tool to people that study and practice in the field of Criminal Justice. It is perhaps the most central instrument that is used in the discipline.
