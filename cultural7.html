Conclusion

Snoop Dogg’s song “No Guns Allowed,” brings up the issue of gun control. It is one of the most controversial issues in the world of politics today. In the song, Snoop Dogg says that there should be “no guns allowed.” This is probably not the best solution as it infringes on people’s constitutional right. There should be some background checks as far as acquiring guns, but people should still be allowed to own them. With these solutions violence should decrease over time. However, in this world there will always be violence, and even with the most strict gun control laws possible there will still gun violence.
