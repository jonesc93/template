Executive Summary

In this paper I will describe the forms of communication, technology, and research used in the discipline of Criminal Justice. Criminal Justice is a large field in which many people study and are employed in. In this disciple many forms of reading, writing, and research are conducted to perform the tasks assigned. These forms of communication are essential to people who practice within the discipline. People that practice within the field of Criminal Justice conduct research every single day. Research is critical to finding out information that is valuable in investigations and other projects. One of the most practiced and most valuable forms of research would be reading. Reading plays a large part in the discipline of Criminal Justice. Technology also plays an important role in Criminal Justice. Technology is extremely important for police officers to perform their duties. It assists them with tasks and also makes things much easier than in the past. Technology also greatly increases productivity in the field of Criminal justice. Lastly, communication is essential to people that study and practice in the field of Criminal Justice. It is probably the most important tool that is used in the discipline. It is crucial for officers as well as students to have exceptional communication skills.
