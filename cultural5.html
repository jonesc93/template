Proposed Solution

Examining these arguments, for and against gun control, lead me to my proposed solution. I believe that the individual has a constitutional right to posses a firearm. However, I do believe that if that person is a criminal or mentally unstable than they should not be able to own a gun. For this reason I believe that with the sale of each gun there should be a background check done. This includes background checks at large gun conventions where background checks were not previously done.
